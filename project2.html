Beginners Guide to the sport of Cycling and Bicycles

Cycling is a fun sport for people of all ages and sizes. Its a healthy activity that provides many benefits to not only your health but to your community. Their are several styles of cycling too making it diverse for everyone.

There are several kinds of bikes people ride. We will dive into the three common bikes most people ride.

The Three Most Common Bikes

  • Road Bike
  • Mountain Bike
  • Commuter Bike

Types of Bikes:

Road Bike

Mountain Bike

Commuter Bike

These are three most common bikes cyclist use throughout their daily lives. The Raod bike is for going fast on paved roads and can be used in competive races. The Mountain bike is needed in rough terrian in the mountains and for climbing up and down trails. The Commuter bike is used for more personal activites such as getting to work, running errands, and going to the local cafe.

Picking a bike can be challenging at times. With today's current economy, the price of completed bikes and frames has gone up. Making sure you pick the right one for your size and needs is important!

If you are looking to hit the outdoors and ride some trails with some descents then the Mountain bike will be your choice.
